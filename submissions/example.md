## 1. Define

_What question are you trying to answer, or what output are you trying to produce?_


## 2. Find

_Where does the data live? (spreadsheets, emails, paper, shared drives, people's heads...)_


## 3. Get

_How do you actually collect or extract the data? How long does it take?_


## 4. Verify

_How do you check that what you collected is correct and complete?_


## 5. Clean

_What needs fixing before the data is usable? (duplicates, inconsistent formats, missing values...)_


## 6. Analyse

_What do you do with the clean data? Who does it?_


## 7. Present

_What does the final output look like? Who receives it?_
